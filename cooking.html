Cooking

I like cooking, especially momo. Momo is one of Nepal’s most popular dishes, which can be eaten as an entree or as a main dish. It is a dumpling filled with meat or vegetables as well. It is eaten with tomato pickle. It’s one of my favorite things to do, and I love it.
